Green Chutney

Recipe courtesy of Frank, 2000

1 cup fresh mint

1 cup fresh cilantro

1 or 2 green chilies

½ sweet onion

¼ tsp cumin

¼ tsp salt

1 tsp sugar

2 tsp lime or lemon juice

 

Combine all ingredients, except juice, in a food processor.  Lightly process. Add juice and stir.  Add salt to taste.

 

This is a good accompaniment to Samosas (see page 1).

 

 Yields  4 servings
